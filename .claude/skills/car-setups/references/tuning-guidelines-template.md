# Tuning guidelines template (seed for the Notion page)

This is the friendly template used to create the **global** `Tuning guidelines` page in
Notion (`Car setups / {Game} / Tuning guidelines`) during bootstrap. It's written for a
non-technical user: plain language, examples, fill-in prompts. The setup generator reads it
and **your notes here override the shipped defaults**.

> When seeding the Notion page, copy everything below the line. Keep the headings; replace the
> *italic example prompts* with your own preferences (or delete the ones you don't care about).
> The more concrete you are about what you like, the better the generated setups fit you.

---

# 🎛️ Tuning guidelines

These are **my** preferences. The setup generator follows them on top of its built-in
knowledge, and prefers what's written here when they disagree. Per-car notes (on each car's
page) and a specific stage description beat these general notes.

## General driving style
*How do you like the car to feel overall? A few plain-language lines.*
- *e.g. "I like the rear to rotate gently on throttle, but I hate snappy, sudden oversteer."*
- *e.g. "Prioritise stability and predictability over outright agility — I make fewer mistakes."*
- *e.g. "I brake late and trail-brake into corners."*

## Likes / Dislikes
*Things to always lean toward, and things to avoid.*
- **Like:** *e.g. "a planted front end on turn-in", "being able to left-foot brake to rotate".*
- **Dislike:** *e.g. "a floaty car under braking", "understeer on corner exit", "twitchy over bumps".*

## Per surface
*Surface-specific preferences. Leave blank to use the defaults.*

### Tarmac
- *e.g. "Run it stiff and low, I want it on rails."*

### Gravel
- *e.g. "Softer and higher than you'd think — I cut a lot and clip ditches."*

### Snow / ice
- *e.g. "Maximum stability and traction; I'd rather be safe than fast."*

## Anything else
*Constraints, habits, or notes that don't fit above.*
- *e.g. "I don't use the handbrake much.", "Keep ABS off on everything."*
